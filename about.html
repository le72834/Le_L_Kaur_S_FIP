A few words our store

The Original Belgian Wheat Beer. Almost 600 years ago in the Belgian village of Hoegaarden, a band of monks experimented by adding oddball botanicals from halfway around the world to their traditional wheat beer. Our recipe, inspired by their creation, is a beer with a refreshing flavor and captivating aroma, unlike any in the world. Let the refreshing citrus and earthy coriander alleviate the stresses of modern living and whisk you to another place entirely. You might call it divine inspiration... we call it Cowbell Brewing Co..

Establishment

Establishing Cowbell Brewing Co. in Huron County is an exciting adventure. Each step of the way has a story behind it. We want to share this and other stories of our remarkable community and our destination brewery through tours of our brand new facility.

First Partnership

Cowbell Brewing Co. manufactures batteries in the USA, Europe and China and provides a network of worldwide sales and distribution centers to serve your business.

Almanac

The very first Almanac Anniversary Series beer was brewed at Cowbell on the opening day in 2017, then faithfully brewed on subsequent anniversaries to commemorate that year in the life of the brewery.
